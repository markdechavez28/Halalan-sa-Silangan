Nagkakaisang Tugon Party Alliance

Nagkakaisang Tugon Party Alliance is a political party in University of the East-Caloocan which aims to provide trained and passionate leaders.

Ten of the 11 positions are up for election. As a representation of the student body, the Tinig candidates are composed of students from 1st to 4th year from the seven programs of the College of Engineering.
