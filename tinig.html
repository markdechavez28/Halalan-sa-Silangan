Selected General Plans of Actions

This link directs you to the complete GEPOA of the Tinig candidates for the College of Engineering.

Student Affairs

Sulong Komite

Also known as the Engineering Student Council Committee, is the governing body of the Engineering Student Council and works as its progressive leaders.

ENsured Quality

Routine inspections and monitoring of the Engineering Department's facilities and laboratories are among the main objectives of ENsured Quality..

State of the Student Address

Serves as a formal congress for communication and dialogue between the student body and college officials.

Campaign and Activities

TOFI Fight

The continuous fight against Tuition and Other Fees Increase aims to ensure every student's right to access affordable and quality education.

Scholarship Fight

Through sustained advocacy efforts, the slate endeavor to abolish the 7% scholarschip quota imposed by the institution.

UE Surge

A pro-student advocacy designed to guarantee that every student's rights are valued and acknowledged in the university.

Academic Services

ACADEMS

The significant enhanced and efficient utilization and dissemination of transcription reviewers, will be facilitated through collaborative efforts between the council and the Transes Committee.

Seminar and Workshop Series

A holistic approacg towards addressing concerns and issues, emphasizing innovation and excellence in education, with a focus on student's rights and welfare.

OJT Assistance

Involves supporting students as they embark on their On-the-Job Training journey. This includes guidance and resources to help them navigate the demands and expectations of their trainings.

Council Activities

EN Cares

EN Cares is dedicated to assisting students in need. Through donation drives and fundraising initiatices, the council aims to offer support to students encountering challenges.

Ensaya sa Silangan!

A weeklong series of welcoming programs and activities for Engineering students and provides them with an opportunity to socialize and take a break from academic challenges.

ENtegrative Expo

A career expo to offer students a series of activities aimed at empowering their knowledge and skills.
